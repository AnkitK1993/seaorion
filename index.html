Who We Are

About SEAORION

"Just as Orion navigates the night sky, we navigate the complexities of global freight — with precision, clarity, and resolve."

SEAORION is an independent shipbroking and freight forwarding company headquartered in Mumbai, India. We specialise in the chartering and broking of tankers for bulk liquid cargoes — including petrochemicals, clean petroleum products (CPP), vegetable oils, and gas. Our customer base spans ship owners, commodity manufacturers, and international traders across the Middle East, Indian Subcontinent, Southeast Asia, Far East, and West.

Drawing inspiration from Orion — the hunter's constellation that has guided seafarers for centuries — we operate with proactive responsiveness, deep market knowledge, and an unwavering commitment to delivering value to our clients. Our 24×7 operations desk means our clients are never without support, regardless of time zones or trading windows.

Our Services

Comprehensive shipbroking and freight management, modelled on the world's best tanker broking practices.

🚢
Tanker Chartering & Ship Broking
Our core. We broker barges for STS transfer in Far East Asia, small and handy tankers loading from the Middle East, and MR tankers for West-loading cargoes. Trusted by owners, traders, and manufacturers across the globe.
CORE SERVICE
📋
COA & Time Charter Management
Full management of Contracts of Affreightment and Time Charter arrangements — from negotiation and fixture recap through to final performance and renewal. We safeguard your contractual position at every stage.

Demurrage Claims & Collections
Precise laytime calculations, demurrage statement preparation, claim filing, and recovery collections. We navigate charter party clauses, vessel logs, and port records to protect your financial position against unjust delays.

Post-Fixture Operations
Once a fixture is confirmed, our qualified operations team takes over — 24×7. Voyage instructions, charter party compliance, port coordination, bunker supply, disbursement account review, freight and hire collection. Always on desk.
